Our Work

Our team along with volunteers and doctors are always first to respond to natural or manmade disaster, with principal response efforts focused on food, shelter, water, health and sanitation. We reached out to far off villages during 2013 Uttarakhand floods with emergency relief. We continue to work in flood affected areas of Jammu and Kashmir.
